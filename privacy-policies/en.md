# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 March 2026
Language: English (en)
Status: Final

Color Picker & Text Styles Extension ("the App") are designed to work locally on your device.

## Summary

- No account creation is required.
- No analytics SDK is used.
- No advertising SDK is used.
- No personal data is sold or shared for advertising.
- Core features work without sending your data to remote servers.

## Data We Process

The App may process the following data locally on your device:

- Picked colors (for recent color history)
- UI preferences and extension state
- Selected text style properties from the active tab when you explicitly run text inspection

This data is used only to provide app features and is stored in local extension storage.

## Data Collection and Transmission

- We do not collect personal information on our servers.
- We do not require login or account identifiers.
- We do not transmit picked colors or inspected text style data to external APIs for core functionality.

## Permissions and Why They Are Needed

- contextMenus: add right-click actions (Pick Color, Text Properties)
- nativeMessaging: communicate with the macOS host app for native color sampling
- activeTab: run inspection only on the tab you actively use
- storage: store recent colors and settings locally
- scripting: execute user-triggered inspection logic in the current page

## How User Control Works

- Color sampling starts only after your explicit action.
- Text style inspection runs only after your explicit action.
- You can disable or uninstall the extension at any time from Safari settings.

## Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you clear browser/extension storage, reset the extension, or remove the app.

## Children

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be published at this location with an updated date.
